Welcome to BIMS

Bhubaneswar Institute of Management and Science (BIMS) is a premier management institute committed to creating global leaders. With a legacy of excellence, BIMS offers a rigorous academic environment blended with industry exposure, fostering innovation, leadership, and ethical values.

Why Choose BIMS?

  • Academic Excellence: Industry-aligned curriculum designed by experts and academicians.
  • Expert Faculty: Learn from a team of PhD holders, researchers, and industry veterans.
  • 100% Placement Assistance: Proven track record of placements in top MNCs, banks, and corporate houses.
  • State-of-the-Art Campus: Modern air-conditioned classrooms, digital library, computer labs, and wi-fi enabled campus.
  • Holistic Development: Focus on soft skills, personality development, and extracurricular activities.

Programs Offered

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA) - 2 Years Full Time (Dual Specialization)
  • Integrated MBA (IMBA) - 5 Years (After 12th)
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) - 3 Years

Admission Process

Admission to BIMS is strictly based on merit. Candidates must appear for national or state-level entrance exams.

  1. Entrance Exam: Valid score in OJEE / MAT / CAT / XAT / CMAT / ATMA.
  2. Group Discussion (GD): Assess communication and analytical skills.
  3. Personal Interview (PI): Evaluate personality and career goals.
  4. Final Selection: Based on academic record, test score, and GD/PI performance.
